# ScopeGuard — AI Scope Creep Detector

**The first AI tool purpose-built for scope creep detection.** Paste your contract, analyze client messages, and catch scope creep before it costs you $6K-15K/year.

🔗 **Live:** [https://atalovesyou.github.io/scopeguard/](https://atalovesyou.github.io/scopeguard/)

## The Problem

73% of freelancers lose money to scope creep. The average loss is $7,800-$15,600/year. Until now, there was no dedicated tool to detect it.

## Features

- **Contract Auto-Parser** — Paste your SoW, extract deliverables, exclusions, timeline, revision limits automatically
- **Semantic Creep Analysis** — Client messages analyzed sentence-by-sentence against your contract scope
- **5-Category Detection** — Flags creep across features, design, timeline, budget, and deliverables
- **Scope Creep Score** — Instant 0-100 score per message with severity breakdown
- **Pushback Scripts** — AI-generated responses in 3 tones: professional, firm, collaborative
- **Client Tracking** — Per-client creep averages, counts, leaderboard of worst offenders
- **PDF Scope Reports** — Professional PDF export with stats, client breakdown, category analysis
- **CSV Export** — Full log export for your records
- **Dashboard** — Weekly trends, client leaderboard, quick stats
- **100% Client-Side** — Your contracts never leave your browser
